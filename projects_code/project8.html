DDE_UOW_Works4

Amid the Ukraine conflict, this artwork conveys the unsettling reality of possible nuclear warfare today. It highlights the tension between progress and peril, urging us to confront our responsibility in preventing such a catastrophe. I decided to continue with a collage style for the first piece, using a hand to represent the overwhelming superpowers of both the United States and Russia with their nuclear capacity. For the hand, I started by drawing the hand and importing it. The background is a simple two tone image that I used the wind filter on. The bottom image is a photo I pulled from a 1982 issue of National Geographic. I then overlaid the two houses with yellow and blue colours. This is intended to represent Ukraine as an underdog against Russia. I aimed to convey the fragility of our world in the face of possible nuclear warfare. The first collage uses a hand to represent the United States and Russia's nuclear power, with a background signifying the turbulent global environment. The second piece forms a word mosaic in the shape of a mushroom cloud, serving as a stark reminder of the potential consequences.
